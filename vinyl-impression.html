About Vinyl Impression

At VI we are an energetic group of creative problem solvers. Our mission is to create spaces people love, using large format printed graphics and signage for events of all shapes and sizes. Our work stretches over a broad range of sectors, from corporate to charity, press launches to awards dinners. Wherever and whatever your event, we are sure to help. Get in touch today. We also offer a special service to event Venues, where we become your branding partner, creating branding documents, design templates and photoshop mockup files to help show your clients what options they have for branding in the venue. Interested in this Free service? Get in touch today.
